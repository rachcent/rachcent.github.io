Phase 6:Breadboard Quiz

Phase 6:Microbox

Made a kirby themed box and used the song Green Greens and Gourmet Race. Using midi tones, it reminds me of chiptune music and so I wanted to use a song from a video game. I initially wanted to use a different song but it didn't turn out well after making it a midi and was hard to recognize the notes. I used a shoe box as the enclosure for the micro box. The most difficult part of making the device work was the photocell. I had to go into office hours to ask about how to use the photocell to make change the pace of the song. When I took the bread board quiz, It took me a while to figure out I was actually doing it correctly, but because of my potentiometer, I thought there was a mistake on my end. When I would turn the potentiometer, the music would get distorted. I decided to make the box into Kirby because the character is able to absorb and copy abilities as well as change into different objects. I placed the speakers, potentiometer, and button on the painted stars.
