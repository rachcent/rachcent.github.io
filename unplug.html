Unplug, 2017

Found Footage, Adobe Premiere, Music Video
Running Time: 2 min, 36 seconds
I felt really inspired to create a music video after hearing the song “Datademon” by Lalaplux.This piece conveys the overconsumption of media and obsession of technology. In addition, there is so much content on the internet, that you can find yourself lost in there for hours.The endless amount of gifs, internet art, memes, and stock footage stays on the internet forever. I felt that sometimes, we should find time for ourselves to unplug from the always-connected internet world.
