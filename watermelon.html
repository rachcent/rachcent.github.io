Watermelon, 2018

Adobe Premiere, Video
This video is about the desensitization of violence and gore that we see in movies, cartoons, video games, and on television. It also questions to what extent do we draw the line to put this type of content out there for the public to see. For others, violence and gore can be offensive and truly triggering for others to look at. Others find themselves intrigued and drawn to seek thrill and suspense because of the violent and gory content we see in media.
